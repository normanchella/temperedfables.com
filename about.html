About Tempered Fables

Fictional short stories, each tempered to leave you with a lesson to remember.

Tempered Fables is a collection of short fiction spanning fantasy, comedy, horror, and romance. Every story is written and narrated under the pen name N.T. Cloever — a quiet corner of a much larger world.

Part of Eterna

Tempered Fables is the fictional podcast that started it all — the seed of the Eterna garden. It lives in Eterna's Wisteria poetic layer: the place where stories are told for their own sake, and where a fable can carry more truth than a fact.

This site is deliberately lean. If you want the full world — the lore, the connections, the wider garden — that lives at Eterna. If you just want the stories, you're already home.
